Banh Mi

Description

Source: Wikipedia. In Vietnamese cuisine, bánh mì or banh mi is a short baguette with thin, crisp crust and soft, airy texture. It is often split lengthwise and filled with savory ingredients like a submarine sandwich and served as a meal, called bánh mì thịt. Plain banh mi is also eaten as a staple food.

Ingredients

  • French baguette
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cilantro
  • Boneless chicken breast
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepared chicken breast
  2. Slice the baguette
  3. Spread mayonnaise and other sauce on the baguette
  4. Enjoy the banh mi!
