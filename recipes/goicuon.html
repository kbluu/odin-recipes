Goi Cuon

Description

Source: Wikipedia. Gỏi cuốn, salad roll, summer roll, fresh spring roll, spring roll, or rice paper roll, is a Vietnamese dish traditionally consisting of pork, prawn, vegetables, bún, and other ingredients wrapped in Vietnamese bánh tráng.

Ingredients

  • Rice papers
  • Rice vermicelli
  • Shredded cooked chicken
  • Cucumber
  • Fish sauce

Steps

  1. Fill rice paper with warm water
  2. Top off rice paper with chicken and cucumber.
  3. Enjoy with fish sauce!
